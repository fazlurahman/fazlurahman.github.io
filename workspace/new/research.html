Research

My research primarily focuses on the following areas:

  • Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) Studies
  • Galactic Emissions and their role in cosmology
  • Development of component separation pipelines for CMB data analysis
  • Exploring novel tools for studying random fields in cosmology

I work on developing techniques and pipelines that can help in deciphering the most detailed and complex datasets from cosmological observations.
