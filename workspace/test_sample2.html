I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Mitchell Institute for Fundamental Physics and Astronomy, Texas A&M University. I work in observational cosmology with Prof. Kevin Huffenberger. My research interests include the statistics of Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) and Galactic emissions, developing component separation pipelines for CMB experiments, and exploring novel tools and techniques to study random fields, among other topics.
