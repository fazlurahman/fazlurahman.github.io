I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Mitchell Institute for Fundamental Physics and Astronomy, Texas A&M University. I work in observational cosmology with Prof. Kevin Huffenberger. My research spans different aspects of Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) observations and using them to study the physics of the early universe. Below are additional details.

Research Interests

  • Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) & Early Universe Physics
  • CMB Data Analysis & Component Separation
  • Modelling Galactic Emissions
  • Random Fields in Astrophysics and Cosmology
    • For more details on my recent works, please see the Publications page.
